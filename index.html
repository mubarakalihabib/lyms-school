About Our School

LYMS is committed to academic excellence, moral values, and holistic development. We nurture confident, responsible, and future-ready learners for success in every field.

Our Curricula

LYMS follows the national curriculum while offering a diversified, inclusive program that nurtures students’ talents and prepares them for careers in applied sciences, technology, humanities, and commerce. As an International College, LYMS provides opportunities for further education through a comprehensive preparatory curriculum.
